# Core Loop And Player Journeys

## Loop

1. Drive to discover POIs and changing terrain biomes.
2. Interact with POIs to gain resources and complete quests.
3. Trigger timed pulse rewards every 2-4 minutes.
4. Convert resources into upgrades and journey level progress.
5. Capture postcard moments and continue another run.

## Player Journeys

- Relax
  - Goal: smooth low-pressure cruising.
  - Pulse: slower cadence, gives cooked food and scenic score.
  - Best for: long sessions and screenshot-focused players.

- Collect
  - Goal: maximize resource efficiency.
  - Pulse: medium cadence, gives wood and stone bonus.
  - Best for: progression and quest completion players.

- Challenge
  - Goal: faster pace and skill pressure.
  - Pulse: faster cadence, gives temporary speed growth.
  - Best for: mastery-focused and challenge players.

## In-Game Controls

- `1` relax journey
- `2` collect journey
- `3` challenge journey
- `J` cycle drive preset
- `P` mark share moment
- `F2` toggle KPI panel
