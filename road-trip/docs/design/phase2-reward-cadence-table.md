# Phase 2 Reward Cadence Table

## Target Completion Cadence

- Micro missions: every 3-6 minutes
- Mid missions: every 12-20 minutes
- Weekly missions: 1-3 completions per long session cycle

## Reward Bands

- Micro
  - Primary: 10-14 coins
  - Secondary: small material or food bonus
- Mid
  - Primary: 28-35 coins
  - Secondary: growth axis boost or speed/gear gain
- Weekly
  - Primary: 120-140 coins
  - Secondary: growth level increase and scrap bonus

## Anti-Stall Rules

- Tier pool exhaustion triggers tier-local mission rotation reset.
- Active slot duplicate prevention remains enforced during reroll.
- At least one mission in each tier should be reachable without rare outcomes.
